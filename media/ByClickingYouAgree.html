One-plantAnimal

By Clicking You Agree (2021)




It is the year 2021, and the word "AI" still has its utopian edge. It takes a full day to generate only a few minutes of caustic, hazy audio. Out of the indistinct cloud of digested rave viscera comes a funny murmur of almost-words, almost-singing, almost-drumming.
