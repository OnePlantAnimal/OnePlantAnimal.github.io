Mammal (2023-2024)




Mammal is a new member of the family of mouth-resonator devices including the Sonovox (realized recently in the Electrospit), Talkbox, Kustom's The Bag, and the electrolarynx. The tube of the talkbox is exchanged for a method of coupling inspired by the Vietnamese string instrument Dan K'ni. Tension in a string transfers sound from a transducer to a small membrane, much like a paper cup telephone, which can then be used to project audio into the mouth. This prototype sounds similar to a commercial talkbox, but is notably quieter.
