Ultrasniffer (2023)




Ultrasniffer is your typical EMI sniffer ala the work by Nick Collins, Christina Kubisch and others, the LOM Elektrosluch, or the Make Mag Elektrosluch project with one twist: there is a sample-and-hold which allows one to heterodyne ultrasonic EMI waves back down into the audible range. The result mostly still sounds like an EMI sniffer with some extra squelchy aliasing artifiacts, although ocassionally tones not audible with a normal sniffer are apparent with the ultrasniffer.
