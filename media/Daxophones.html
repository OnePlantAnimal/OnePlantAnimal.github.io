Daxophones




The Daxophone is a bowed idiophone invented by avant-luthier Hans Reichel. Some time during high school (around 2021? I can't remember) I built one. Since then, I've been playing here and there in a rock band, for a christmas album, and for various free music ensembles. I play a quiver of my own tongues, as well as tongues from the excellent Daniel Fishkin, and a Dax cantilever + tongues from John Jansen.
